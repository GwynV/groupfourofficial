What is Database System?

Database Systems or DBMS is software that caters to the collection of electronic and digital records to extract useful information and store that information is known as Database Systems/ Database Management Systems or DBMS. The purpose of a standard database is to store and retrieve data.


What is Data Modelling?

Data modelling is process of creating a data model, without the process, developers will work closely with stakeholders (i.e., potential users of the to-be-created system) Data modeling begins with collecting information about business requirements and rules from stakeholders.


What is Information Management?

Information management is the process of acquiring, organizing, storing, and using information. The goal is to ensure that information is delivered to the right audience at the right time, to the right place, and in the right format in an efficient and effective manner.
